About Me

This page is meant to be an informal introduction of who I am. I am currently a second year undergraduate at IIT Bombay doing a B.Tech in Engineering Physics. My main interests currently are Quantum Information and Computing, Quantum Simulation and Quantum Measurement Theory. I am currently a teaching assistant in the Department of Physics, IIT Bombay for the course PH 107 which is taught to all first year undergraduate students.
I am an avid math and physics enthusiast and am currently working as a convener in the Math and Physics Club, IIT Bombay. I was also the moderator for the Quantum Computing Workshop that was held by the MnP club and was involved in carrying out the interactive sessions and designing the modules for the worskhop. You can find all material for that workshop here.

In my free time I listen or make music otherwise play video games or watch shows. I would say my favorite show of all time is Neon Genesis Evangelion which is nothing short of a masterpiece in my opinion and I would highly recommend that to anyone who is a fan of phsychological shows. While the show does have it's flaws it is enjoyable and definitely impactful. I enjoy playing retro games quite a lot and am generally a big fan of the aesthetic of these games. Super mario 64 is my favourite game of all time and I am currently playing through The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time.
